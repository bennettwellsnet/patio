# Patio Builder Guide

An interactive demo guide to building a beautiful, long-lasting paver patio — with special emphasis on the critical pre-work and foundation preparation that determines whether your patio lasts 5 years or 25+.

**Live demo:** https://bennettwellsnet.github.io/patio/ (or the published version at bennettwells.net/patio once set up)

## Focus Areas
- Why foundation work is 80% of a successful patio
- Proper excavation, base materials, and compaction
- Drainage and slope (the #1 cause of failures)
- Step-by-step with practical tips and calculators

Built as a self-contained static site.

## Local Development
Just open `index.html` in a browser. No build step required (uses Tailwind Play CDN + vanilla JS).
